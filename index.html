I am a Ph.D. student at Human-Computer Interaction lab in Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), and under supervision of professor Feng Tian. My research interests are physiological computing for Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and new interaction devices and methods. I interned in Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) with Human-Computer Interaction Group during the second year of my Ph.D. to work on prototypes of interaction hardware and software.

In 2011, I got my bachelor's degree in Beihang University (BUAA), and my major is computer science. When I'm in Beihang University, I interned in Tsinghua University to begin the exploration on HCI.

I am a Ph.D. student at Human-Computer Interaction lab in Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), and under supervision of professor Feng Tian. My research interests are physiological computing, wearable computing and new interaction devices and technology for Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I interned in Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) with Xiang Cao and Human-Computer Interaction Group during the second year of my Ph.D. to work on prototypes of interaction hardware and software.

In 2011, I got my bachelor's degree in Beihang University (BUAA), and my major is computer science. When I'm in Beihang University, I interned in Tsinghua University with professor Yuanchun Shi to begin the exploration on HCI.
